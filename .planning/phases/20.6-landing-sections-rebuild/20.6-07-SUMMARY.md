## Decisions Made

### Lamp-Decision: REPLACED (not polished)

**Chosen:** Option B — Replace with DS-native closing-visual.

**Rationale:**

Lamp-Effect required too many DS-violations to salvage:

1. **Easing hardcoded** — `ease: "easeInOut"` five times in `lamp.tsx`, not `var(--ease-out) = cubic-bezier(0.16,1,0.3,1)` from DS §E.
2. **Duration/delay hardcoded** — `delay: 0.3, duration: 0.8` across four motion.divs, not `--dur-normal/--dur-slow` tokens.
3. **Layout magic numbers** — `scale-y-110 sm:scale-y-125`, `translate-y-40 sm:-translate-y-52`, all outside `--space-*` grid.
4. **Ad-hoc animation stack** — Conic-gradient + 4-stack motion.divs + `blur-2xl`/`blur-3xl` compositing = heavy post-FX effect with zero DS token binding. CONTEXT.md D-02 explicitly forbids: *"Keine Ad-hoc-Animationen ohne DS-Rückbindung."*
5. **min-h-screen default** forced `min-h-[70vh]` override, which in turn forced mobile scroll distance without content justification.

Token-swap fix would have needed: full rewrite of motion.div transitions (4×), drop of conic-gradient in favor of radial-gradient with DS tokens, removal of scale-y/translate-y layout, swap of blur stack. At that point = full rewrite. Replacing with a single-layer DS-native closer is strictly smaller surface.

**Replacement — Signal-Beam Closer:**

- **Vertical accent beam** growing from bottom via `scaleY: 0 → 1, origin-bottom`, duration 0.9s `--ease-out`. Height `min(240px, 28vh)`. Carries the roter-Faden motif vertically — narrative echo to Hero LabeledNodes (which carries horizontal red-thread connections).
- **Radial-gradient backdrop** — `ellipse 70% 55% at 50% 100%` using `var(--accent-glow) → rgba(var(--bg-rgb), 0)`. Subtle glow (opacity 0.35) that evokes the Hero-Constellation stimmung without glassmorphism or heavy blur.
- **Horizontal accent hairline** top of section, `scaleX: 0 → 1` entry, gradient fade on both sides. Echoes the Signal-Grid horizontal accent-thread.

Total: 3 motion-layers using exclusively DS tokens, vs. old 4-motion-divs + conic-gradient + 2 blur-layers with zero token binding.
