## Decisions Made

- **TOOLS_URL env-name choice:** Used `TOOLS_URL` (not `E2E_BASE_URL`) so the tools-app spec runs against `localhost:3001` independently of the default `BASE_URL` (which defaults to `https://tools.generation-ai.org` per `playwright.config.ts`). This separates the tools-app test concerns from the general Playwright baseURL — Plans 07-09 can pin tools-app to `localhost:3001` for dev or override via `TOOLS_URL=https://preview-...` for CI/Vercel-Preview without disturbing other specs.

- **`test.fixme()` over `test.skip()`:** `fixme` semantically signals "expected to fail / not yet implemented" while `skip` signals "intentionally skipped indefinitely". Using `fixme` makes future intent explicit and would surface if a developer accidentally fills the test body without removing the `fixme`.
